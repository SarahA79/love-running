Why love running with us?

Feel healthy

Since it improves aerobic fitness, running is a great way to improve your cadiovascular health. Plus, it burns caloris and can build strength.

Feel strong

Running strengthens your heart by pumping blood more effectively through your body.

Feel free

Physical exercise, running included, is known to release endorphins in the brain.
Endorphins make you more awake and, yes, happier.

get social

When we run with others, we can run further and faster more easily. Social running can pull you out of a running funk or low mood, and inspire you to try new events.
